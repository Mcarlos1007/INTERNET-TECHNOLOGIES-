FACTS ABOUT CATS


Cats are small, independent animals that many people keep as pets. They are known for their soft fur, sharp claws, and excellent ability to hunt small animals like mice. >
Cats are very flexible and can jump high or squeeze through small spaces. >
Most cats enjoy sleeping for many hours during the day and become more active at night. >
They also communicate with humans by meowing, purring, and using their body language. >
Because they are clean animals that groom themselves often, cats are generally easy to care for and are loved by many people around the world. 🐱
